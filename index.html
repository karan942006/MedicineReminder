The Problem

Medication Adherence Shouldn't Be This Hard

Patients, caregivers, and families all face the same gap — no reliable way to track, verify, and coordinate medicine schedules.

🧓

Missed Doses, Real Risks

Patients managing chronic conditions juggle multiple daily doses, stock counts, and dosage sizes — a single missed reminder can mean a missed dose.

📵

The Caregiver Blind Spot

Long-distance family members have no visibility into whether an aging parent or child has taken their medicine, short of repeated phone calls.

🧩

Fragmented Family Schedules

Multi-dependent households juggle separate routines for every family member, with no single place to see who's taken what, and when.

What is MedReminder

The reminder that rings even when the world is offline.

MedReminder schedules alarms directly on your Android device, so they fire on time whether or not you have a signal. Patients get a clean daily scheduler with stock tracking; caregivers get a real-time window into compliance — no manual updates required.

FAQs

Common Questions

Does MedReminder work without internet?

Yes — alarms are scheduled locally on your device using Android's AlarmManager, so they fire on time even if you're offline.

How does a caregiver connect to a patient?

The caregiver switches to Guardian Mode, enters the patient's unique Telemetry Link Code, and taps Couple Sync Stream to start receiving live updates.

Is the app available in Marathi?

Yes — MedReminder offers an instant translation toggle between English and मराठी throughout the interface.

What happens when stock runs low?

An amber refill warning banner appears automatically once a medicine's stock count drops to 5 pills or fewer.
